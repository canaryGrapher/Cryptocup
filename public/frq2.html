Question 2

The British Royal Family has been betrayed by the German Nazi Factions. An urgent strategy exchange needs to happen between The British and their allies. All communication channels are being monitored by The Germans. They need to formulate a new encryption method that allows for safe transmission of information. They do so by modifying the information in the following ways:


  1. Put the information (plaintext) in a square matrix with size = floor(sqrt(strlen(pt))+1) followed by {'_','a','b','c','d','e','f','g','h','i',[[[NOT 'j']]],'k','l','m',..............'x','y','z'} and repeat the same sequence.

  2. The cipher text is formed by traversing the matrix diagonally in alternating up and down directions. Eg. If matrix is
        abcd
        efgh
        iklm
        nopq

      Then the ciphertext is :
        aebcfinkgdhlopmq 

            EXAMPLE

            Plain Text (PT)- 
            escape_from_here
            

            Plain Text to Box -
            e s c a p
            e _ f r o
            m _ h e r
            e _ a b c
            d e f g h
            
            Cipher Text -
            eesc_me_faprh_deaeorbfgch
